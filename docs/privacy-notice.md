# Privacy Notice Draft

Hangul Gesture IME Research is a clean-room research keyboard for closed beta testing.

## What stays on the device

- Keystrokes, Hangul composing state, English shift state, and gesture decisions are processed locally.
- The app does not request network permissions.
- The app does not store typed sentences, passwords, clipboard contents, contacts, account data, or identifiers.
- Touch correction stores only local offset statistics when a typed key is immediately deleted. The stored value is an aggregate position bias, not the text that was typed.
- Users can reset touch correction from the app settings with `입력 보정 초기화`.

## Data sharing

The app does not transmit app data to the developer, third parties, analytics services, ad networks, or cloud services.

## Retention and deletion

Local keyboard settings and touch correction statistics remain on the device until the user changes settings, resets correction, clears app storage, or uninstalls the app.

## Closed beta disclosure text

This keyboard processes input locally on your device. It has no network permission, does not save typed text or passwords, and stores only local touch correction statistics derived from immediate delete patterns.
